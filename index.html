Who are We?

Siobhan Boylan is a National Board Certified teacher who taught in K-5 public, charter, and private schools for over 15 years. After quitting in March 2022, she created Teachers2Techies, where teachers and recruiters connect and combine the magic of teaching with the magic of technology!
